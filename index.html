WHO WE ARE

We Are tBP/Architecture

tBP/Architecture is a full-service architectural practice specializing in facilities planning and design for educational and public sector facilities in California. Since our inception in 1952, we have been dedicated to helping our clients to provide thoughtful, quality design to support and facilitate student success. Our primary focus has been on educational facilities, K-12, college and university facilities; 95% of our projects are K-14 Educational Projects.
